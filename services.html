Hypnosis

Hypnosis is a trance-like state of focus and concentration achieved with the help of a clinical hypnotherapist. In this state, you can turn your attention completely inward to find and utilize the natural resources deep within yourself that can help you make changes or regain control in certain areas of your life. Hypnosis can be used to help treat anxiety, phobias, substance abuse including tobacco, sexual dysfunction, undesirable spontaneous behaviors, and even bad habits among many other things. Three sessions are recommended for optimal results.

The goal is to guide and support you in developing and integrating mindfulness into your daily life, ultimately bringing you more peace.
